WELCOME TO HYDERS BIRYANI

Welcome to Hyders Biryani, your ultimate destination for authentic and mouth-watering South Indian cuisine. Located in the heart of Navalur, we pride ourselves on serving the finest biryanis, rich in tradition and flavor, alongside a diverse menu of soups, starters, and special dishes crafted to perfection. Whether you're craving a hearty mutton biryani, a spicy chicken starter, or a flavorful vegetarian option, our dishes are prepared with the freshest ingredients and a passion for excellence. Join us for a culinary experience that celebrates the true essence of South Indian flavors in a warm and welcoming atmosphere.

About Us

At Hyders Biryani, we believe that food is more than just a meal—it's an experience. Founded with a passion for authentic South Indian cuisine, our restaurant in Navalur is dedicated to bringing the rich, traditional flavors of the region to your table. Every dish we serve is a testament to our commitment to quality, crafted with the finest ingredients and a deep respect for culinary heritage.

From our signature biryanis, infused with aromatic spices and cooked to perfection, to our wide array of soups, starters, and main courses, we aim to offer something special for every palate. Whether you're a fan of the spicy kick of our Chicken 65 or the comforting warmth of our mutton dishes, our menu is designed to satisfy and delight.

But Hyders Biryani is more than just great food. It's about creating a space where families and friends can come together to share a meal, celebrate moments, and make memories. Our warm and welcoming atmosphere is a reflection of our values—hospitality, integrity, and a love for good food.

Join us at Hyders Biryani and experience the taste of South India, served with a side of warmth and tradition.
